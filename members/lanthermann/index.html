Cyprien Lanthermann

Post-Doctoral Researcher

CLASSIC++/CLIMB++: Upgrade of the CLASSIC/CLIMB interferometric beam combiner

Detectors -- Interferometry -- Optical Design -- Polarimetry


Current Research Summary:

CLASSIC++/CLIMB++ consists in the upgrade of the existing CLASSIC/CLIMB beam combiner situated at the CHARA array. It will use the e-APD detector technology to focus the improvement in sensitivity of the instrument, by upgrading also its optical design to adapt to the new camera and improve even more the performances.

Prior Research:

Post-Doc at KU Leuven - Early-stage study of the MARVEL facility
PhD at IPAG - Avalanche detectors for astronomical near-infrared interferometry: application to the multiplicity of massive stars
