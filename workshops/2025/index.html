NYRIA WORKSHOP 2025

Heidelberg, Germany

Date: 22nd - 26th September 2025
Venue: Max Planck Institute for Astronomy
Local Organizers: NYRIA 2025 MPIA
Application: Application form

Travel support for US-based applicants to attend NYRIA 2025
NYRIA 2024 (held at UC San Diego) have limited funds to support US-based applicants for international travel. If you are at a US-based institute and funding limitations are a reason you may not be able to attend NYRIA 2025 in Germany, please apply and send an email to sperera(at)ucsd.edu stating your name, institute and career stage, by the NYRIA application deadline. Further information/steps will be provided.

It is our pleasure to announce the in-person NYRIA Workshop 2025! We will celebrate NYRIA's 10th aniversary by returning to Heidelberg, where the first workshop was held in 2015. The workshop will take place from the 22nd to 26th September 2025.

The workshop is designed to encourage early career researchers, (i.e. masters students to senior postdocs) working in ground-based, visible and infrared instrumentation for astronomy to engage with each other in a friendly environment. Attendees will have the opportunity to present their work, network with peers, and develop collaborations with other participants from institutes around the world. The workshop will include a series of talks from each participant in addition to a few talks from invited speakers, a tour of local astronomical facilities, and a hackathon in which participants will work together to solve an instrumentation-themed problem.

The application fee includes the full program, four nights of accommodation, transportation from the accommodation to the venue, lunch for three days, a workshop dinner and tea and coffee breaks

Application Start: March 27rd 2025
Application Deadline: May 23rd 2025
Notification of Acceptance: Mid of June 2025
Registration Fee: 110€
Contact information: If you have any questions please contact us at nyria(at)mpia-hd.mpg.de
